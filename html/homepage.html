Why is it so great?

You can use this tool to boost collaboration and foster communication. Teamify tells you what's being worked on, who's working on what, and where something is in a process. The team can be managed by the team leader and the managers can manage different projects, it can also be used for personal use - more like a list-making/to-do tool. Projects progress can be tracked and deadlines can be set. It makes assigning tasks and managing the team much easier. Make team management a smooth experience with Teamify.
